 Note: The Uttaradhyayana Sutra is contained in Sacred Books of the East Vol. 45, Part 2.
This volume also contains the Sutrakritanga Sutra. Together with Vol. 22 Part 1, these
two volumes contain the major SBE translations of Jain canonical texts.

 Notes 
 The Uttaradhyayana Sutra is one of the most important Jain scriptures. It contains
36 lectures on various aspects of Jain doctrine and practice, delivered by Mahavira and
other Tirthankaras. The text covers topics including the soul, karma, liberation,
asceticism, and moral conduct.
