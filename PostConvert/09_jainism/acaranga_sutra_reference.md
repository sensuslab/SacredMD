 Note: The Acaranga Sutra is contained in Sacred Books of the East Vol. 22, Part 1.
This volume also contains the Kalpa Sutra. The SBE translation by Hermann Jacobi
is the classic English translation of this foundational Jain text.

 Notes 
 The Acaranga Sutra (Sanskrit: Acaranga Sutra; Prakrit: Ayaranga) is the first of
the twelve Angas (limbs) of the Jain canon. It contains the teachings of
Mahavira, the 24th Tirthankara of Jainism, and prescribes rules for Jain monks.
