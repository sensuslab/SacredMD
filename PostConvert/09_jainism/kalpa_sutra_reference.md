 Note: The Kalpa Sutra is contained in Sacred Books of the East Vol. 22, Part 1,
alongside the Acaranga Sutra. The Kalpa Sutra portion covers pages 217-320 of the
volume and contains: Lives of the Tirthankaras (including Mahavira), Life of Parsva,
Life of Arishtanemi, Epochs of the intermediate Tirthankaras, Life of Rishabha,
List of the Sthaviras, and Rules for Yatis.

 Notes 
 The Kalpa Sutra is a Jain text containing the biographies of the Tirthankaras,
notably Parshvanatha and Mahavira, as well as the rules for monks. It is traditionally
attributed to Bhadrabahu and is one of the most important texts in the Svetambara
Jain tradition.
