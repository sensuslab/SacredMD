ARCHIVE NOTE
============

The Qolasta (also spelled Qolastā) is the canonical prayerbook of the Mandaeans.
On gnosis.org, this text is presented under the title "Ginza Rba" (also spelled
Ginza Rabba), which translates to "The Great Treasure" or "Great Book".

The Qolasta and Ginza Rba refer to the same collection of Mandaean prayers
and liturgies. The full text has been downloaded from gnosis.org and is
available alongside this file as:
  - ginza_rabba_ginza_rba.html (Full HTML)
  - ginza_rabba_ginza_rba.txt  (Plain Text)

ABOUT THE QOLASTA
=================

The Qolasta is the principal collection of Mandaean religious poetry and prayers,
containing the baptism liturgy, masiqta (death mass) prayers, daily prayers
(nishmata), hymns of praise, and other liturgical texts.

The version on gnosis.org is excerpted from:
  E.S. Drower, Canonical Prayerbook of the Mandaeans, Leiden: 1959
