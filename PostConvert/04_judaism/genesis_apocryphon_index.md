TEXT COVERAGE:
--------------
The Genesis Apocryphon retells Genesis narratives from Lamech/Noah (cols. 0-17) 
and Abraham/Lot (cols. 18-22). The manuscript is fragmentary.

COLUMN SUMMARY (from Reeves translation):
- Col. 0 (fragments): Lamech and Noah cycle
- Col. 1: [fragmentary]
- Col. 2: Lamech questions Batenosh about Noah's paternity
- Col. 3-5: Enoch's revelations to Methuselah about Noah
- Col. 6: Noah's autobiography - birth and marriage
- Col. 7: Noah's vision of the Flood
- Col. 8-9: [largely missing]
- Col. 10: Noah's sacrifice after the Flood
- Col. 11: God's covenant with Noah
- Col. 12: Noah's dream vision of the olive tree (vineyard)
- Col. 13-15: Noah's division of the earth among his sons
- Col. 16-17: Geographical description of the divisions
- Col. 18-19: Abraham in Egypt, Sarah and Pharaoh
- Col. 20: Abraham's description of Sarah's beauty; Pharaoh takes her
- Col. 21: Abram and Sarai leave Egypt; battle of the kings (Genesis 14)
- Col. 22: God's promise to Abram; Melchizedek
