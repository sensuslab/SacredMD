ACCESS NOTES
================================================================================
The B-numbers (e.g., B-284281) are image catalog IDs in the Leon Levy Digital
Library. Each B-number corresponds to a photographic plate that may contain
one or more manuscript fragments. The manuscript associations shown on each
page indicate which Dead Sea Scrolls manuscripts appear in that photograph.

The image viewer on the digital library uses a JavaScript-based viewer that
loads images dynamically. The screenshot captures show the image viewer pages
as displayed in the browser, which is the publicly accessible form of these
images. Direct high-resolution image downloads are restricted by the image
viewer's architecture.

For scholarly transcriptions and translations, consult:
- Discoveries in the Judaean Desert (DJD) series (Oxford University Press)
- The Dead Sea Scrolls Reader (Brill)
- Online: Orion Center for the Study of the Dead Sea Scrolls
